Why the old page feels dated
Visual age
The composition reads like an early-template Wix build: lots of empty space without intention, limited hierarchy, and no premium visual rhythm. It feels built before the post-2022 bar for beauty and salon brands.
Trust gap
The site says the salon focuses on Afro-Caribbean hair textures and bridal beauty, but it does not build enough luxury or specialist trust around that claim on the page itself.
Broken polish
There are small confidence leaks: the Facebook icon points to Wix, the booking CTA has a spelling issue, and the address data is inconsistent between schema and contact-page content.
Conversion
The current structure does not guide a new visitor clearly from “What is this salon known for?” to “Why trust them?” to “What should I book?”
