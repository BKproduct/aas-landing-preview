FAQ

DIY stack vs working with you?

ChatGPT plus ad-hoc Zaps gets you experiments. We deliver owned workflows with monitoring, documentation, and clear handover — so the automation survives Monday morning.

How do you handle data access?

We work with the access you grant; secrets never belong in git. We can start read-only where that makes sense for your risk posture.

What about AI regulation?

You remain the deployer for your domain. We help implement controls proportionate to risk — we do not offer legal certification.
